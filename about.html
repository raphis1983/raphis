About me...

​Hi! My name is Rafal Szczytko, I live in Poland and create illustrations using digital art. All my works I sign under the nickname "Raphis" or inverted "R".
In my artworks you can usually find a reflection of an alternative reality. I use elements of surrealism, abstraction, science fiction, pop art and retro. I'm trying not to limit myself but rather experiment with different types of 2D and 3D techniques.

The tools I use: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Blender 3D, GrafX2.
